❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Before contacting us, you may find quick answers in our FAQ section.

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in any course by visiting the course page and clicking the "Enroll Now" button. Follow the payment process, and you'll get immediate access to the course materials.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and popular digital wallets. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

How can I access course materials after enrollment?

Once enrolled, you can access all course materials through your student dashboard. You can watch videos, download resources, and take tests from any device with internet access.

Do you provide certificates after course completion?

Yes, we provide digital certificates upon successful completion of all course requirements, including assignments and final assessment. Certificates can be downloaded from your dashboard.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 7-day refund policy from the date of enrollment if you're not satisfied with the course. Please contact our support team with your refund request.
